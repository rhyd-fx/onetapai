## Split is a defender-sided vertical map
Split funnels attackers through tight chokes with heavy verticality (Heaven,
Rafters, Vents). Mid control — Mid Mail, Mid Vent, and the ropes — is the key to
splitting either site, but Mid is a maze of angles that punishes dry pushes.
Attackers must use utility to take Mid methodically; defenders can hold Mid
aggressively because rotations are short. Because chokes are tight, coordinated
utility (smokes + flashes + a molly) matters more than raw aim on Split.

## Split A and B site execution
A Site is defended from Heaven, Screens, and Rafters — clear the high ground with
recon/flashes and smoke off Heaven before entry. B Site sits behind B Main and is
watched from Rafters and Alley; smoke Rafters and clear the close corners.
Post-plant, extend to take map space and hold crossfires rather than standing on
the spike, since defenders retake through predictable chokes you can pre-aim.

## Split recommended agents
Sentinels and controllers dominate Split. Killjoy is borderline S-tier — turret
and Lockdown lock sites and Mid. Viper is a strong situational pick (especially in
a double-controller setup) for walling Mid and sites. Raze thrives clearing the
tight spaces with Boom Bot and nades; Sage walls buy time on the chokes.
