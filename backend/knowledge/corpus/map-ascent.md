## Ascent mid control decides the map
Ascent is an open two-site map whose wide Mid is the single most important area.
Mid connects to both sites via Market (to B) and Tree/Cat (to A). Whoever controls
Mid dictates rotations and can split a site instead of hitting Main into stacked
utility. Attackers should invest smokes and a flash on Mid Arch/Catwalk early;
defenders should contest Mid with an Operator or trade setup, because giving up
Mid for free lets attackers default into either site with tempo. The
openable/closeable site doors let executing attackers cut rotations — close them
on entry.

## Ascent A and B site fundamentals
On A, the key duel is Main vs. defenders in Generator, Heaven, and Tree. Use a
flash or recon to clear Generator before committing; hold Heaven/Garden if you
lose early control and play the retake. On B, Market and B Main are the two
entries — coordinate so utility hits site as bodies arrive, and use the site door
to isolate the fight. Post-plant, hold from Lamps/CT and Market crossfires rather
than sitting exposed on default plant.

## Ascent recommended agents
Killjoy is borderline top-tier here — Turret plus Lockdown defines how sites get
held on defense. Sova and Fade give the recon needed to pry open Mid and A. A
controller who can wall/one-way Mid (Omen, Viper, Clove) is high value, and Jett
or an Operator holds the long Mid/A angles.
