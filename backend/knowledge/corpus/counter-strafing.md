## The problem: moving while shooting
In Valorant, moving tanks your accuracy — bullets spray wildly unless you are
standing still (or crouch-creeping). Dying in a duel while dealing zero damage is
the classic signature of shooting mid-strafe: you peeked, pulled the trigger
while still sliding, and every bullet missed.

## Counter-strafing
To stop instantly, tap the opposite movement key. Moving right (D), then tapping
left (A) zeroes your velocity in a few milliseconds, giving you a pinpoint first
bullet. The rhythm is: strafe out, counter-tap, shoot the instant you are static,
then strafe back behind cover.

## Practice pattern
- **ADAD discipline:** in the Range, strafe A, counter with D and fire only when
  fully stopped. Repeat with the opposite direction. Reward yourself only for
  first-bullet head shots, not sprays.
- **Zero your velocity before every shot.** Say it out loud while practicing until
  it becomes automatic.

## How to diagnose it from stats
A high rate of rounds where you died having dealt zero damage (a "zero-damage
death") strongly implies you are peeking while still moving. If more than ~25% of
your death rounds are zero-damage, prioritize counter-strafing over aim training.
