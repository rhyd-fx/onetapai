## Close Range Engagement Tactics (< 10m)
At close range, movement speed and fast tracking are more important than fine micro-adjustments.
- **Spray & Run-Gun Control:** At distances below 10m, full spraying is viable. Do not stay stationary; run-and-gun is viable with SMGs (Spectre/Stinger) or pistols (Frenzy/Ghost) at very close ranges. If using a rifle (Vandal/Phantom), drag down hard on the first 5 bullets while moving laterally to track the target's chest/head.
- **Crosshair Placement:** Position your crosshair at head level, pre-aiming close corners where defenders might hold passive angles. Swing wide to throw off enemy crosshair placement if you know their position.

## Medium Range Spray and Burst Fire (10 - 20m)
Medium range requires disciplined shooting mechanics: spraying beyond 5-7 bullets is highly inaccurate.
- **Bursting and Spray Pull-Down:** Limit your firing to 3-5 round bursts. After the first 3 bullets, the recoil rises vertically; pull down gently on your mouse. After 6 bullets, recoil moves randomly from left to right; stop shooting, reset, and burst again.
- **Strafing and Bursting (Movement Invalidation):** Integrate counter-strafing. Press A to run left, tap D to stop immediately, shoot a 3-bullet burst the instant your velocity drops to zero, then press D to run right, tap A to stop, and shoot again. This makes you a difficult target while maintaining 100% first-bullet accuracy.

## Long Range Precision & First-Bullet Accuracy (> 20m)
Long-range duels are won by first-bullet accuracy, crosshair placement, and absolute discipline.
- **Tapping and Micro-Adjustments:** Never spray at distances greater than 20m. Shoot only 1 or 2 bullets at a time, aiming strictly for the head. Allow the recoil recovery time (approximately 0.3 seconds) to fully complete before shooting again.
- **Aim Down Sights (ADS) Viability:** ADS reduces weapon fire rate but eliminates spread and recoil by 20-30%, making it highly viable for holding long lanes (e.g., Icebox A-Screen, Haven C-Long).
- **Correcting Low Headshot Percentage:** If your HS% is below 15%, you are lazily aiming at chest level or spraying too early in the duel. Keep your crosshair aligned with head-level geometry (box tops, wall stripes) at all times.
