## Duelist Mastery: Jett and Miks Playbook
Duelists are entry-fraggers responsible for taking space, pathing into sites, and winning opening duels.
- **Jett Entry Pathing:** Never execute onto site without coordinating with initiator utility. Dash into smoke (Cloudburst) placed on site to disrupt enemy crosshair placement. While inside the smoke, clear close angles first, then swing with teammates. Keep your Tailwind dash active when holding aggressive angles with an Operator to secure a pick and escape instantly.
- **Miks Entry & Space Creation:** Use active movement abilities to clear entry angles. Do not dry-peek default defenders; wait for flash/recon support. Coordinate secondary entry trades with your sentinel or initiator.

## Sentinel Mastery: Vyse, Chamber, and Veto Defensive Setups
Sentinels anchor sites, lock down map areas, and deny enemy space or flanks.
- **Vyse Utility Anchoring:** Place traps at main choke points. Delay entry using shear/slow mechanics rather than fighting multiple enemies head-on. Coordinate with your team's smoke controllers to isolate targets behind your wall.
- **Chamber Aggressive Hold:** Chamber plays as a hybrid sentinel-duelist. Position your Rendezvous anchor behind secure cover. Hold aggressive, off-angle lines with the Headhunter or Tour de Force. Take a single shot and teleport out immediately. Do not over-stay your angle; your job is to create a numbers advantage and fall back.
- **Veto Anchor Setups:** Place detection utility at back-site entries or lane links. Play defensively, using cover to stay alive and delay the enemy spike plant. Use crossfires with your active traps to secure easy defensive kills.

## Initiator and Controller Integration: Tejo and Role Guidelines
Initiators gather info and flush out defenders, while Controllers manipulate sightlines with smokes.
- **Tejo Intel Gathering:** Deploy recon utility deep into site entry points (e.g. Ascent A-Main, Bind Showers). Do not guess enemy positions; wait for scan confirmation before calling a site execute.
- **Smoke Placement (Controllers):** Always smoke off primary defender rotation lanes (e.g., Heaven, Screens, Spawn links). Make sure smokes are flush with the walls so defenders cannot flash out of them safely. Never place "deep" smokes that allow defenders to take space inside the choke point.
