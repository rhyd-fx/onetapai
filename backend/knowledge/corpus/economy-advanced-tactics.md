## Buy Tiers and Loadout Optimization
Managing your team's economy is critical to locking down consecutive round wins.
- **Eco Rounds (< 1,000 Credits):** Save money to ensure a full buy next round. Do not buy rifles or heavy shields. Buy light shields and a cheap pistol (Sheriff or Ghost), or save completely. Play close corners together to secure a weapon drop from an over-committed enemy.
- **Half Buy / Semi-Buy (1,500 - 2,500 Credits):** Balance your buy so you have at least 3,900 credits for the following round. Buy light armor and a cheap weapon (Spectre, Marshal, or Sheriff).
- **Force Buy (2,500 - 3,900 Credits):** Buy down to 0 credits when you must win the round (e.g. anti-matchpoint, or after winning pistol round). Buy heavy/light armor and a secondary tier rifle (Bulldog or Guardian) or SMG.
- **Full Buy (> 3,900 Credits):** Buy heavy shields, full abilities, and a primary rifle (Vandal or Phantom) or Operator.

## Preventing Eco-Throws and Securing Anti-Eco Rounds
Eco-throws happen when a fully bought team loses to an enemy team that has pistols or light buys.
- **Anti-Eco Playstyle:** When playing against an enemy eco/pistol round, never take close-range fights. Sheriff/Shorty are lethal at close range but useless at long range. Play long-range lines, keep your distance, and group up. Never dry-peek corners one-by-one.
- **Weapon Safety:** If you die, call out the weapon location instantly so a teammate can cover or retrieve it. Allowing an eco team to secure a Vandal/Phantom is the primary cause of eco-throws.
