## Breeze is the big, long-range Operator map
Breeze has the largest open spaces in the pool and the longest sightlines, so
rifles and especially the Operator dominate. Close-range agents struggle here.
Attackers must respect long angles — never dry-peek an Operator hold across open
space; use smokes to cut sightlines and cross open areas together after utility.
Mid and the wide A/B mains are contested primarily at range, so first-bullet
accuracy and pre-aimed head-height crosshair placement matter more on Breeze than
anywhere else.

## Breeze A and B site fundamentals
A is large and open (A Main, A Halls, Cave/Pyramids) — smoke the long defensive
angles and use fast utility to take the big site space, then hold the plant from
multiple extended positions. B is entered through B Main and Mid/Window with a
long back-site; wall or smoke the deep angles before committing. Because sites are
huge, post-plants favor whoever holds the most crossfire angles — spread out and
trade.

## Breeze recommended agents
Jett is the premium pick — still the best Operator user, with Updraft/Tailwind
angles nobody else reaches; on Breeze with an Op she is a top individual play.
Viper is close to mandatory for walling the huge open sites. Skye/Sova provide
recon and flashes to cross the open ground, and a second controller helps cut the
long lines.
