## Sunset is a compact, Mid-centric two-site map
Sunset is a smaller two-site map (A and B) where Mid is short and directly
contestable, making rotations fast and Mid control decisive. Because the map is
compact, taking Mid lets you split either site quickly and cut defender rotations;
losing Mid gives defenders the same fast access to your flank. Utility usage is
tight and impactful — a single smoke or flash on Mid or a Main choke often decides
the round.

## Sunset A and B site fundamentals
A is entered via A Main and Alley/Elbow and defended from Boathouse and Tiles;
smoke the long angle and clear Elbow before entry. B is taken through B Main and
Market with a Mid split; smoke the deep angles and use the short rotations to hit
before defenders stack. Post-plant, extend off the plant and hold crossfires,
since the compact layout means retakers arrive fast through predictable chokes.

## Sunset recommended agents
Mid control agents shine: a smokes controller (Omen, Clove) to split Mid, and a
recon initiator (Fade, Sova, Gekko) to clear the Mid and Main angles. Cypher or
Killjoy secures the flank on this fast-rotating map. Duelists (Raze, Neon) use the
tight spaces and short rotations to entry and re-aggress quickly.
