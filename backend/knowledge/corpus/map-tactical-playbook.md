## Bind: Teleporter Playbook and Site Executes
Bind has no mid, making lane control on A-Bath, A-Short, B-Long, and B-Hookah crucial.
- **A-Site Executes:** Duelists should entry through A-Short, while a support player pushes Bath (Showers). Controllers must smoke off Heaven and Lamps (U-Hall). Initiators should scan the back-site and trucks. Use the B-to-A teleporter for rapid rotations if B defense is heavily anchored.
- **B-Site Defense:** Sentinels should set up inside Hookah or behind B-Long containers. Hookah is a high-risk choke point; delay entry with mollies or slows, and coordinate crosshairs with a teammate playing cubby or back-site.

## Ascent: Mid Control and Door Mechanics
Ascent is defined by its wide-open Mid area and closeable doors on both A and B sites.
- **Mid Control (A-Link / B-Market):** Control of Mid is vital. Defenders should pre-aim Mid Link and Catwalk. Attackers should smoke off Mid arch or Catwalk to split the defense. If Mid is won, execute a split push onto B through Market or A through Short.
- **Site Retention:** On A-site, close the tree door instantly when the enemy executes to cut off rotators. Play retake from Heaven if you lose site control early; do not die anchoring a site alone.

## Haven: 3-Site Defenses and Lotus Lane Defaulting
Three-site maps require flexible rotation setups and active lane defaults.
- **Haven C-Site Defense:** C-Long is a long-range Operator lane. Position a sentinel trap in C-Garage link, and let your Operator hold C-Long from Back-Site or Platform. Rotate early if Garage is breached.
- **Lotus Defaulting:** Default on A-Rubble and C-Mound to gather sound cues before committing to a site push. Rotate through the rotating doors to catch defenders off-guard during rotations.
